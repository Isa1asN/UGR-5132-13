History

Etorithm initially was just a telegram with only few subscribers and was used to share just few ebooks and anouncements. Also it used to be called Athenaeum Academy. The name Athenaeum was used in relation to the functions it served to its subscribers i.e. providing books. It did not have its own admins and its own website.

The overall activity was all low and had few posts per week. And even the channel did not get enough attention and even it stopped posting or sharing new items. and the number of its subscribers decreased drastically. Originally, the focus of Athenaeum was just fresh students of AAU/AAiT and sharing materials and the like. The channel still exists and it has only 1.2k subscribers and is still used by Etorithm.

Mission

Etorithm provides important courses like in programming, web development, UI/UX design , and other related areas of the software engineering field via ebooks, video tutorials, audio and also providing a discussion area on telegram for students to learn from each other and ask questions.



It aims to provide education freely and easily accesible to anyone interested and who want to develop their skills and get a job.Students can easily access all the contents using their laptop with out any payment and can learn. They can get the courses video tutorials, the ebooks the informations they need about the courses and the discussion area on telegram to communicate with their colleagues and friends.
